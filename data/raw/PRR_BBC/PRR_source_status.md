## Conclusion

**A primary BDA cost document (DPR, Government Order, board resolution, or official
cost estimate PDF) for the Peripheral Ring Road / Bengaluru Business Corridor could
NOT be located publicly as of 2026-06-06.**

The DPR for PRR-2 was being commissioned via consultancy tender as recently as
February 2024 (BDA/2023-24/SE0010), suggesting the PRR-2 DPR may not yet be
complete or public. The PRR-1 (Phase 1) tender notices are on Karnataka e-procurement
portal but the portal was inaccessible for automated download.

The cost figures most usable for P1 research (₹27,000 crore total; ₹21,000+ crore land
acquisition; ₹3,348 crore for Package 1 construction) are attributable to BDA officials
and have appeared in multiple credible financial news sources (Moneycontrol, Deccan
Herald, Indian Express) but must be cited as secondary sources.
